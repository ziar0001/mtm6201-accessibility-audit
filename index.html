Our story

Brew & Gather started in 2019 as a small pop-up in the Glebe neighbourhood of Ottawa. We wanted to create a space that felt less like a transaction and more like a ritual — a reason to pause, connect, and be present.

Today we're at the corner of Bank and Clemow, open seven days a week. Come find your corner.
